A Website For You

Hi Aiden, I have decided to continue working on the website I intended to program for you. Unfortunately after our break up, I had deleted a lot of the information used to program your website. I even stopped the process because it hurt too much to think of you. As a result, I am not yet done with it. I will update this every day, especially with my daily letters. I will continue to update with more song lyrics that remind me of you, and add some drawings of mine as photos on here. When we're back together, we can add some more photos and memories to our scrapbook. This website will live on. I will be writing you more poems as time goes on, and you can simply search my website for you if you're ever thinking of me. I'm always here for you. I love you.


I hope you like your website. Think of it as a birthday gift for you, since I'll be publishing it on May nineteenth after all. Have a great sixteenth birthday. If you see the 'send a message' section: I'll be working on that in the next few days. If you ever want to send me your journal entries, or daily summaries of your day, I'll read them. You may start writing them now, if you wish, though I won't see them yet. I will soon. I'll read every word you write me.

I'll be seeing you soon.
